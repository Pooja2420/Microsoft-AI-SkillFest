Management center overview
Article
2025-02-28
2 contributors
In this article
The management center is a part of the Azure AI Foundry portal that streamlines governance and management activities. From the management center, you can manage Azure AI Foundry hubs, projects, resources, and settings. To visit the management center, open theAzure AI Foundryand (while in a project) select theManagement centerlink from the left menu.

Manage hubs and projects
You can use the management center to create and configure hubs and projects within those hubs. UseAll resourcesto view all hubs and projects that you have access to. Use theHubandProjectsections of the left menu to manage individual hubs and projects.

For more information, see the articles on creating ahubandproject.
Manage resource utilization
You can view and manage quotas and usage metrics across multiple hubs and Azure subscriptions. Use theQuotalink from the left menu to view and manage quotas.

For more information, seeManage and increase quotas for resources.
Govern access
Assign roles, manage users, and ensure that all settings comply with organizational standards.

For more information, seeRole-based access control.
