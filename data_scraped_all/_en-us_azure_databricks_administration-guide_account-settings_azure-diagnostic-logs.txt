Azure Monitorâs diagnostic settings service does not log all of these services. Services that are unavailable on Azureâs diagnostic settings are labeled accordingly.
Note
Azure Databricks retains a copy of audit logs for up to 1 year for security and fraud analysis purposes.

Diagnostic log schema considerations
If actions take a long time, the request and response are logged separately but the request and response pair have the samerequestId.
requestId
Automated actions, such as resizing a cluster due to autoscaling or launching a job due to scheduling, are performed by the userSystem-User.
System-User
TherequestParamsfield is subject to truncation. If the size of its JSON representation exceeds 100 KB, values are truncated and the string... truncatedis appended to truncated entries. In rare cases where a truncated map is still larger than 100 KB, a singleTRUNCATEDkey with an empty value is present instead.
