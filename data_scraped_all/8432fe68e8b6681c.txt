View closed captions in the Azure AI Video Indexer website
Article
2024-10-09
2 contributors
In this article
This article shows how to view closed captions in theAzure AI Video Indexer video player.
View closed captions
Go to theAzure AI Video Indexerwebsite and sign in.
Select a video for which you want to view captions.
On the bottom of the Azure AI Video Indexer video player, selectClosed Captioning(in some browsers located under theCaptionsmenu, in some located under thegearicon).
UnderClosed Captioning, select a language in which you want to view captions. For example,English. Once checked, you see the captions in English.
To see a speaker in front of the caption, selectSettingsunderClosed Captioningand checkShow speakers(underConfigurations) -> pressDone.
