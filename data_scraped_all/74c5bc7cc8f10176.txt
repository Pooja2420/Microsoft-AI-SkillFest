Security controls are built into Azure Spring Apps Service.
A security control is a quality or feature of an Azure service that contributes to the service's ability to prevent, detect, and respond to security vulnerabilities.  For each control, we useYesorNoto indicate whether it is currently in place for the service.  We useN/Afor a control that is not applicable to the service.
