Supported metrics for managed Functions in Azure Static Web Apps
Article
2024-12-30
2 contributors
In this article
Azure Static Web Apps collects a series of metrics when you add a managed API to your application.
To view metrics on your app, open your static web app in the Azure portal and selectMonitoring. In theMonitoringquery window, you have access to metrics that report activity of your application.
Supported metrics
The following metrics are tracked for your application:
BytesSent
CdnPercentageOf4XX
400
CdnPercentageOf5XX
500
CdnRequestCount
CdnResponseSize
CdnTotalLatency
DataApiErrors
DataApiHits
FunctionErrors
FunctionHits
SiteErrors
SiteHits
